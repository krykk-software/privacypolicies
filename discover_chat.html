Privacy Policy for DiscoverChat Mobile App

Updated February 18, 2025

At DiscoverChat, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect from users of our mobile app and how we use, share, and protect that information.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information from users of our mobile app:

  • Personal Information: When you register for DiscoverChat, we may collect your name and email address. We use this information to create your user account and to provide you with access to our features.
  • Usage Information: We may collect information about how you use DiscoverChat, including the pages you visit, the features you use, and the time spent using the app.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about the device you use to access DiscoverChat, including the device type, operating system, and mobile network information.

AI Model Selection and Search Data

Users can select one or two Generative AI (GenAI) models when conducting searches. Search data is passed to the GenAI provider solely for generating a response. Users should not provide confidential or personally identifiable information in their searches.

Subscription and Chat Limits

DiscoverChat offers flexible subscription plans:

  • Limited Subscription: 50 chats per month, access to all AI models, and chat with two models at once.
  • Unlimited Subscription: Unlimited chats per month with all AI models, with no restrictions.

By default, users can engage in up to 20 chats at no cost.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide you with access to our features and improve our app.
  • To communicate with you about our app and provide customer support.
  • To personalize your experience with our app and provide recommendations based on your usage.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties without your consent, except in the following cases:

  • With service providers who help us improve our app.
  • When required by law or in response to legal processes.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of our company, users, or others.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user information from unauthorized access, disclosure, and misuse. However, no system is completely secure.

Your Choices

You can delete your account and associated data anytime by contacting us. You can also opt out of promotional emails through the provided unsubscribe instructions.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify users of material changes via our website or email.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@krykk.com.
